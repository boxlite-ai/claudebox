# YC W26 (Y Combinator Winter 2026) - Complete Research

> Research date: 2026-02-14
> Demo Day: 2026-03-24 (upcoming)
> Batch size: 100+ companies, 132+ founders publicly accepted

## Batch Overview

- YC expanded to 4 batches/year (Winter, Spring, Summer, Fall) starting 2025
- Each batch ~half the size of previous format (~100-130 companies vs ~250-300)
- Investment: $500K total ($125K for 7% equity + $375K SAFE converting at next round valuation)
- W26 runs Jan-Mar 2026 in San Francisco
- Demo Day: Tuesday, March 24, 2026

## Key Themes in YC W26

1. **AI Agents Dominate**: Majority of companies are building autonomous AI agents/workers for specific verticals
2. **Healthcare is Hot**: 10+ companies in healthcare AI, from billing to primary care to drug discovery
3. **Fintech Compliance**: Multiple companies automating fraud, risk, and compliance for banks/fintechs
4. **Robotics Renaissance**: Strong cohort of robotics infrastructure companies (sensors, data, configs, CV)
5. **Space Ambitions**: At least 3 space companies with serious traction (Spaceium's $86M contracts)
6. **B2B > Consumer**: B2B SaaS dominates; very few pure consumer plays
7. **Vertical AI**: Deep industry-specific solutions rather than horizontal tools

## Sector Breakdown (approximate)

- AI / Enterprise Software: ~35%
- Healthcare / Biotech: ~20%
- Fintech / Compliance: ~12%
- Robotics / Hardware: ~12%
